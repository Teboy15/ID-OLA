Yello Garri

Our yellow garri is produced with carefully selected cassava and enriched with quality palm oil to give it a rich colour and appealing taste. It is well processed, properly fried, and neatly sieved to ensure cleanliness and consistency.

This garri is free from stones, sand, and unwanted particles. It is dry and crunchy, not oily or soggy, and does not stain excessively when soaked. The palm oil used is clean and well blended, not overpowering.

Our yellow garri is suitable for soaking, drinking, and preparing smooth eba. It swells properly when cooked and maintains a pleasant aroma.

We ensure proper frying and drying to reduce excess moisture, helping the garri last longer without developing mould or bad odour when stored correctly. No harmful preservatives are added.

Our yellow garri also comes in various sizes, from small 1kg packs to larger 50kg bags, catering to different needs and preferences.
